Why build with Ionic?

Ionic is a dev platform for the entire app lifecycle that allows teams to build better apps, faster.

  • As future-proof as the web

    The apps you build with Ionic leverage the power and stability of the open web - the most time-tested universal runtime in the world. The web has been around over two decades, and we believe it will be there for the next two. Always bet on the web.

  • Backed by a global community

    Ionic is backed by a vibrant community of more than 5M developers in over 200 countries. Join the thousands of global meetups, forums, and community driven events that make it easy to learn and grow with our platform.

  • Rooted in Open Source

    We’re a company built on open source. It all began with Ionic Framework - the world's most popular open source SDK for building cross-platform mobile and Progressive Web Apps. Our open source roots powers everything we do at Ionic.
