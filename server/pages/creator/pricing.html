Questions?

( If you need more help, just shoot us a message )

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
No. Creator is a pay-as-you-go service, and subscriptions vary from month-to-month or yearly billing cycles. You can upgrade, switch, or cancel your plan at any time.
When am I charged?
Immediately after you upgrade, and then every month, or year, depending on the plan you chose. We offer a money back guarantee if you aren't happy within 30 days of signing up!
Can I cancel at any time?
Of course. There are no obligations or contracts. You can cancel at any time in the billing section of the Dashboard. Once you cancel your account, you will not be charged again.
Can you explain private vs. public projects?
By default, Creator projects can be accessed by anyone with the URL. However, you can protect your projects by setting them to “private” with password protected permissions for better privacy while sharing projects.
How do I pay?
We currently accept all major credit card processors: Visa, Master Card, American Express, and Discover cards. However, at the moment we do not accept PayPal.
How secure is my payment information?
Every single Creator account comes standard with SSL encryption. All payments are securely processed by industry leader Stripe. Your card info never even touches our servers.
Can I pay yearly?
Yes. We're happy to accommodate customers looking for annual billing. Just choose the “Yearly” option when upgrading your account to any of our paid plans.
What is the Creator App?
The Creator mobile app lets you preview and interact with your prototypes as if they were native, without having to compile or download anything. It’s available through the App Store and Google Play Store.
